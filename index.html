Barcelona

Barcelona, Spain is a great place to visit and I definitely reccomend going there to see vibrant city life and the beach.

Places to visit

  • Cuitadella Park
  • Sagrada Familia
  • Poblenou, Barcelona, Spain

Why Visit?

Barcelona is a great place to visit if you want to go on a stress free getaway. They speak Spanish and English. The people there are very kind and helpful.

What should I do at Barcelona?

  • Try La Paella, Spain's national dish. A very great combination of seafood.
  • Go to places by walk, especially if they are around the corner. Parking is pretty limited. The weather is nice as well.
  • Have Ice Cream. Very good flavors!
